## Summary

Money Manager is an **offline, on-device** personal finance app. All of your information stays on
your phone. The app has **no internet access**, **no user accounts**, and **no servers**. We do
**not** collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your data.

## What data the app stores

Everything you enter is stored **only on your device** (in the app's private local database and
settings storage). This includes:

- Loans / EMIs — names, amounts, interest, dates, payment history, and notes
- Expenses and the categories you create
- Your profile name and profile photo (if you add one)
- App settings — theme colour, chosen currency symbol, App Lock preference, and home-screen layout

We do not have access to any of this. It never leaves your device unless **you** choose to export it
(see "Backups" below).

## What data we collect

**None.** The app contains no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third‑party SDKs that
collect personal data. Because the app requests no internet permission, it is technically incapable
of sending your information anywhere.

## Backups (export / restore)

You can manually export a backup file containing your data. This is entirely under your control:

- The backup file is saved to a location **you** choose on your device using the Android file picker.
- If you share that file (for example over WhatsApp or email), you are choosing to share it — the app
  does not do this automatically and has no ability to.
- Restoring a backup reads a file you select and replaces the data on your device.

## Permissions the app uses

- **Notifications** — to remind you before a loan/EMI payment is due.
- **Biometric (fingerprint/face) / device credential** — only to unlock the app if you turn on App Lock.
  Your biometric data is handled by Android and is never seen by or stored in this app.
- **Run at startup (boot completed)** — to re-register your payment reminders after the phone restarts.

The app does **not** request access to the internet, your contacts, your location, your camera, or
your microphone.

## Security

- If you enable **App Lock**, the app requires biometric or device‑PIN authentication to open.
- The app sets a "secure window" flag, which prevents its contents from appearing in screenshots or
  the recent‑apps preview.
- Automatic system cloud backup of the app's data is disabled.

## Data retention and deletion

- Your data remains on your device until you delete it.
- You can delete individual records (expenses, loans) inside the app at any time.
- **Uninstalling the app permanently removes all of its data from your device.**

## Children

This app is a general‑purpose personal finance tool and is not directed at children. It collects no
personal information from anyone.
